United nation goal 7

The goal I would like to solve is having affordable and sustainable energy. I will take action on this goal by researching energy and trying to support the United Nations.

On the United Nations website, many things can be seen, like the target of universal access to modern energy by 2030 or increasing the global percentage of renewable energy by 2030. Other goals the UN has set up include doubling the improvement in energy efficiency by 2030 and promoting access to research, technology, and investments in clean energy by 2030.

I believe all of these goals can be achieved if everyone starts taking action by doing a number of things such as donations, not using multiple devices at the same time, turning off air conditioning while sleeping, switching off appliances at the socket, buying rechargeable electronics, and staying informed using the many links supplied by this website.

This is not a Offical United Nations website.
